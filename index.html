Attention, Cozylanders!

03-06-2026

We appreciate the loyalty of this wonderful community, but the development team regrets to inform you that we will be discontinuing the project next month. We have been experiencing technical issues beyond our control, and we believe it is best to be transparent with the many of you who not only played as children but have also seen your own children carry on the legacy of your magical avatar. Cozyland’s domain will soon disappear, but the memories we've made will live on forever.

- CozyTownRoyalty

About the deleted private and forum messages

05-03-2025

It has been brought to our attention that some user data has been compromised because of a breach in the security of the server. We lament any inconvenience this has caused to our players and we assure that the problem has been fixed and dealt with. We recommend from now on to backup any important conversations or messages. Thank you for playing CozyTown!

- CozyTownRoyalty
